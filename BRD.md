## 2. Buiness Overviw
The organizarion operates in a multi-region retail environment and generates large volumes of transactional sales data daily. However, decision-makes currently lack a unified view of business performance, reulting in delayed insights and reactive decision-making.
This project aims to develop a centralized, interactive Sales Analystics Dashboard that transforms raw sales data into actionable buinsess intelligence.

---

## 3. Problem Statement
Business often struggle to understand:
  Which product are performing well
  Which regions generate highest revenue
  Monthly/Yearly sales trends
  Customer purchasing behavior

This project solves these issues using visual analytics.
