# Executive Overview

The **Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard** was developed to provide business stakeholders with a centralized, interactive reporting solution for monitoring organizational performance across products, customers, regions, and time.

The solution consolidates transactional sales data into standardized business metrics, enabling executives to evaluate revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance through a single source of truth.

By combining SQL-based analysis with Power BI visualization, the project transforms raw operational data into actionable business intelligence that supports faster, data-driven decision-making.

---

# Business Challenge

Prior to implementing this solution, business reporting relied on fragmented datasets and manual reporting processes, resulting in:

* Delayed access to business insights
* Inconsistent KPI reporting
* Limited visibility into profitability drivers
* Difficulty identifying high-performing and underperforming products
* Limited understanding of customer purchasing behavior
* Reduced ability to monitor business growth trends

These challenges impacted operational efficiency and delayed strategic decision-making.
