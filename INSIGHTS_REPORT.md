# 1. Executive Summary

This report presents the key business insights generated from the Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard. The objective of the analysis was to evaluate organizational performance across products, customers, regions, and time in order to support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis combines SQL-based business analysis, KPI development, and interactive Power BI reporting to identify performance trends, profitability drivers, operational risks, and business opportunities.

The recommendations presented in this report are intended to support executive planning, sales optimization, and long-term business growth.

# 11. Strategic Recommendations

Based on the analytical findings, the following business recommendations are proposed.

## Product Strategy

* Focus investment on high-performing products.
* Review pricing for low-margin products.
* Investigate products generating consistent losses.
* Expand successful product categories.

---

## Regional Strategy

* Increase investment in high-growth regions.
* Investigate declining regional performance.
* Reallocate sales resources where appropriate.

---

## Customer Strategy

* Strengthen retention programs for high-value customers.
* Develop targeted campaigns based on customer segments.
* Increase customer lifetime value through personalized offers.

---

## Profitability Strategy

* Monitor discount effectiveness.
* Improve product mix.
* Reduce low-margin sales.
* Optimize pricing strategy.

---

## Reporting Strategy

* Monitor executive KPIs regularly.
* Standardize business definitions.
* Automate dashboard refresh processes.
* Maintain data quality controls.
